What You Are Looking At

This dashboard aggregates anonymized diagnostic results generated by the NO Support Tool during isolated troubleshooting sessions on systems worldwide.

📊

What Data Is Shown

Each session represents a single execution of the tool, capturing network, DNS, and geo-related checks performed at runtime. Results are categorized by severity and summarized to reveal recurring failure patterns and regional anomalies.

🧪

How the Data Is Collected

When the NO Support Tool is run, it registers the operations you ran and the results and sends the results here. Nothing is left behind on clients computers, and no applications or background processes are left on the system after the application is closed.

🎯

Why This Is Useful

By analyzing signals across locations and tiers, this data helps identify recurring environmental issues, surface regional or ISP-specific constraints, prioritize improvements, and highlight pain points requiring deeper investigation.
